2. E1 - 结局画面背景 (ending_bg.png)
-------------------------------------------
文件: assets/textures/backgrounds/ending_bg.png
尺寸: 640×360 px (2x缩放到1280×720)
风格: 简陋像素涂鸦，纯色块+1px黑轮廓

AI提示词:
```
pixel art, crudely drawn MS Paint style,
closed tavern after hours, 640x360,
same stone and wood tavern interior as B1,
using deeper dark colors (#1E1814 dominant),
heavy closed ledger book on bartop center-bottom,
flat amber (#E8A040) decorative pattern on cover,
wall sconces as dark flat blocks (extinguished),

quiet, contemplative, end-of-journey mood,

```

Negative prompt:
```

```


---
生成建议:
---------
1. 每个背景生成5-10个变体
2. 选择最符合像素涂鸦风格的版本
3. 在Aseprite中手动调整:
   - 将颜色减少到24色板
   - 添加/统一1px黑色轮廓
   - 确保纯色填充，无渐变
   - 调整尺寸为精确的640×360px
4. 导出为PNG-24格式，背景不透明
