## 1. 标题画面 — Hearth's Call

**场景**: 酒馆外景，石门紧闭，门缝透出琥珀色暖光。玩家第一次看到这个游戏。

**情绪曲线**: 神秘(前奏) → 好奇(展开) → 温暖(高潮, 门开了的感觉)

### Suno 提示词 (EN)

```
Medieval fantasy instrumental, solo cello opening with deep resonant tones, slowly joined by gentle lute arpeggios and a soft recorder melody. The mood shifts from mysterious and dark to warm and inviting, like approaching a tavern in a dungeon and seeing warm light spill from under the door. Acoustic, intimate, natural reverb. Slow tempo, building gradually to a hopeful, welcoming climax. No drums, no vocals. Celtic folk influence with medieval minstrel atmosphere.
```

### 风格标签

`medieval folk, celtic instrumental, atmospheric fantasy, acoustic, warm, mysterious intro, slow build`

## 2. 白天地图 — Parchment & Plans

**场景**: 摊开的羊皮纸地牢地图，玩家选择采集点、去商店采购、规划体力分配。白天的准备阶段。

**情绪**: 沉稳、专注、一丝冒险前的期待。

### Suno 提示词 (EN)

```
Medieval folk instrumental, gentle lute fingerpicking as the foundation, light hand drum providing a steady heartbeat rhythm, recorder or wooden flute carrying a simple wandering melody. The mood is calm and focused, like studying an old parchment map by candlelight and planning a journey through dungeon corridors. Not tense, not sleepy — quietly purposeful. Acoustic, warm, slightly rustic. Moderate tempo. Loopable, no dramatic climax. Think Stardew Valley morning music meets medieval minstrel.
```

### 风格标签

`medieval folk, lute instrumental, calm exploration, hand drum, rustic acoustic, planning music, stardew valley medieval`

## 3. 酒馆营业 — The Hearth Beckons ★ 核心曲目

**场景**: 夜晚酒馆营业。拖拽材料合成饮品食物，服务冒险者客人。客人来来去去，吧台后忙碌不停。这是玩家停留最久的场景，**必须能循环播放不腻**。

**情绪**: 温暖、热闹、有节奏感、有生气。像壁炉里跳动的火焰。

### Suno 提示词 (EN)

```
Medieval tavern music instrumental, lively but not chaotic. Lute providing a warm rhythmic strumming pattern, wooden flute dancing above with a catchy folk melody, gentle frame drum and tambourine keeping time, occasional cello adding depth and warmth. The mood is cozy and bustling — a busy tavern evening with crackling hearth fire, adventurers sharing tales over mugs of ale, the clink of glasses in the background. Warm amber glow atmosphere. Medium tempo, steady groove, designed to loop comfortably. Celtic folk tavern style, Dragon Age Inquisition tavern songs vibes but purely instrumental. No vocals, no epic swells, just warm and rhythmic and alive.
```

### 风格标签

`medieval tavern, celtic folk instrumental, cozy fantasy, lute and flute, rhythmic folk, dragon age tavern, warm amber, loopable BGM`

### Suno 提示词 (EN)

```
Medieval ambient instrumental, sparse and reflective. Solo lute playing slow, thoughtful melodies with long pauses, occasional cello drone underneath like a sigh. The mood is quiet and contemplative — closing the tavern ledger at the end of a long night, counting coins by dying candlelight, the last embers fading in the hearth. Melancholic but not sad, tired but satisfied. Very slow tempo, intimate, minimal arrangement. No percussion. Think closing time, the quiet after the storm.
```

### 风格标签

`medieval ambient, solo lute, contemplative, sparse acoustic, late night, intimate, cello drone, quiet closing`

**场景**: 30 天后，故事结束。展示最终金币、声望、NPC 结局。可能是好结局（酒馆繁荣、莱恩存活），也可能是坏结局。

**情绪**: 沉淀、感伤中带释然、旅程结束的复杂情绪。

### Suno 提示词 (EN)

```
Medieval fantasy instrumental, opening with a lone cello playing a slow farewell theme, gradually joined by lute and a distant flute. The mood is bittersweet and reflective — the final page of a long story, looking back at 30 days of memories in a dungeon tavern. Warm but tinged with melancholy, like saying goodbye to friends you may never see again. Building to a gentle, emotional crescendo then fading to quiet acceptance. Strings, lute, harp harmonics. Slow to medium tempo. Acoustic, cinematic but intimate. Think epilogue music, journey's end, the last round before closing forever.
```

### 风格标签

`medieval cinematic, bittersweet instrumental, cello and lute, farewell theme, fantasy epilogue, emotional acoustic, journey's end`

## 备选音乐方向

如果纯中世纪民谣不是你要的感觉，以下是三个备选方向，每条主提示词都可以替换风格词：

### A. Lo-Fi Chillhop 方向（现代感、慵懒）

标题画面示例:
> Lo-fi chillhop instrumental, warm vinyl crackle, mellow piano chords, soft boom-bap beat, cozy and mysterious atmosphere. Fantasy tavern vibes with modern production. Slow tempo, intimate.

### B. Dark Ambient 方向（更沉、更地牢）

标题画面示例:
> Dark ambient medieval instrumental, deep cello drones, distant echoing flute, subtle dungeon reverb, mysterious and slightly ominous. Underground tavern deep in the dungeon. Slow, atmospheric, no percussion.

### C. Synthwave 方向（VA-11 Hall-A 致敬）

标题画面示例:
> Synthwave instrumental with medieval fantasy elements, warm analog synths, retro drum machine, dark and cozy atmosphere. Like a cyberpunk bar but in a fantasy dungeon. Medium tempo, atmospheric.

## 音乐统一性检查清单

选曲时确保 5 首曲子之间的统一感：

- [ ] 核心乐器一致（至少 2-3 种贯穿全部曲目）
- [ ] 制作风格一致（都是 acoustic / natural reverb）
- [ ] 情绪曲线合理（标题暖 → 地图稳 → 酒馆热 → 打烊静 → 结局深）
- [ ] 酒馆曲能循环 10 分钟不腻（这是玩家停留最久的曲目）
- [ ] 没有突兀的风格跳跃（不会突然从中世纪跳到电子）
